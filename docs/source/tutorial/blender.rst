Move, Rotate, Scale
=======================

Select the atoms, and press the transform Shortcut, and move your mouse

- ``g``, move
- ``r``, rotate
- ``s``, scale

You can also try:

- ``g``, then ``x``, move along X-axis.

- ``g``, then ``x``, then ``2``, move along X-axis by 2.
